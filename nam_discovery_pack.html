Phases 1, 2, 5 · Internal CMS
Users affected
~55
Pain severity
High
Frequency
Daily
Intake → DRA → status tracking → diary/notes → document management. This is what 55 internal users touch every single day across 3,000 active cases. Getting this right eliminates the largest source of manual effort. The previous vendor failed here because they tried to build everything at once rather than nailing the core loop.
✓ Enables: NAM staff to run real cases end-to-end in the new system from day one

Bottom Line — What to focus on in next week's sessions
Session 1 — Current state
Ask the AE and Case Manager: what does a bad day look like? How do you know a case is stuck? Walk us through a real case from intake to close. These two personas are your MVP anchor.
Session 2 — Future state
Ask Finance: if we got batch hearing billing right first, how much time would that save per month? Ask Shaun: what does a successful mass filing for a new enterprise client look like today? That's the growth scenario to design for.
MVP in one sentence
A case manager can take a PI case from intake to close — without touching myADR — and the finance team can process month-end without a manual reconciliation spreadsheet.
